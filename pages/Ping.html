Diamond AI Actuator

Stage: Alpha

We are aware that AI might not perfect out the needs of performance. Modulating and Filtering the results at edges is a must. Partnering with our associates at Asansol Engineering College we have developed this AI actuator.This uses Kalmann Filter to minimize the gap in AI and acutal -- in Real time. This is currently in Alpha Testing. This codebase is self reliant, future proof and is being battle tested for our ML and AI upcoming projects internally within the team. We are happy to make it available to you, we are planning to have some podcasts WITH YOU once we finalize with the codebase. If you find bugs in codebase please flag an issue to our project at github and we will attend to it.
